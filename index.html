Mission Statement

Beta Kappa Gamma prides itself in its unique effort to promote growth as well as integration within society. The primary goal and essential mission is to strengthen bonds between existing peoples while becoming more active in the community.

Objective

Beta Kappa Gamma's objectives include providing service to the community through things like increasing awareness for world hunger relief, promoting academic scholarship, and creating a strong bond of brotherhood.

Philanthropy

Beta Kappa Gamma has taken on two major philanthropies that are very close and dear to the Baylor community, with the support from organizations such as Multicultural Affairs, Multicultural Greek Council, the Student Government.
